## Tested Behavior

- Explicit detail controls are the only detail triggers.
- Body, Diet, and Training search/order controls are functional.
- Training overview and selected body-area search retain focus and state.
- Body-area day inclusion follows stored training split; card movement and notes are narrowed to the selected area.
- Movement Dictionary provides a return path to Movement Index.
- Data Check detail opens only from its explicit Open action.
- Reduced-motion rules are present.
